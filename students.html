For students

Earn on your own schedule.

Do work you're already capable of, for neighbors you already share a community with. Build a profile, earn real income, and gain experience around your school and extracurricular schedule.

Why students join Assistly.

  • Sign up with your @ihsd.us email — no separate application
  • Browse tasks that fit your skills and schedule
  • Work with background-checked, vetted families
  • Build a profile and earn reviews as you go
  • Get paid directly — cash, Venmo, or Zelle

Founding Pilot Program

Early access, before the public launch.

Student helpers who join the pilot get first access to participating Indian Hill families and job opportunities, plus a direct voice in shaping features before Assistly officially launches.

Getting started

Four steps to your first job.

01

Sign up

Create your account with your @ihsd.us email. An admin reviews and approves it.

02

Browse tasks

See open jobs from background-checked Indian Hill families near you.

03

Apply and match

Apply to tasks that fit your schedule. When a family selects you, messaging opens.

04

Do the job, get paid

Complete the task and arrange payment directly with the family.
