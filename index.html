Built for Indian Hill, by Indian Hill.

Assistly is purpose-built for the Indian Hill School District. Every account — student or family — is verified to be part of the community before access is granted.

Students earn real income doing work they're capable of. Families get help from neighbors they can actually trust. Everyone stays safe.
