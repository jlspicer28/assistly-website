About Assistly

Safe. Local. Built for Indian Hill.

Assistly is a nonprofit, private job-matching platform connecting Indian Hill families with school-verified students — now welcoming members to our founding Pilot Program.

Indian Hill Exempted Village School District

Our mission

Making it easier for neighbors to help neighbors.

Assistly is a Non-Profit organization. At Assistly, we believe finding trusted help in your community shouldn't be difficult. Our platform connects families with reliable, motivated high school students for everyday needs like babysitting, pet care, tutoring, yard work, and more.

As Indian Hill High School Seniors, we believe in creating community. We created Assistly to make it easier for families to find dependable help while giving students flexible opportunities to gain experience, build skills, and earn money around their school and extracurricular schedules. Every connection is built on trust, safety, and community.

Our goal is simple: strengthen local communities by making it easier for neighbors to help neighbors.

Pilot Program

Join the Assistly Pilot Program.

We're recruiting 50 student helpers and 15 Indian Hill families by September 15 to help us test, improve, and shape the future of Assistly before our official launch.

Why Join the Pilot?

By joining the Assistly Pilot Program, you'll have the unique opportunity to help build a platform designed specifically for the Indian Hill community. Your feedback will directly influence new features, improve the user experience, and help us create the safest and most effective platform possible.

For Indian Hill Families

  • One full year of free access to the Assistly platform.
  • Early access to trusted student helpers before the public launch.
  • The opportunity to provide feedback and help shape the future of Assistly.

For Student Helpers

  • Early access to participating Indian Hill families and job opportunities.
  • The chance to build your profile and gain experience before the official launch.
  • A voice in creating a platform built by students, for students.
No cost to participate. The only cost to you as a participating family is what you pay your helper for the services rendered. Other than that, there are no interest fees, subscription fees, or membership costs for anyone participating in the pilot program. All we ask is that you use the platform and share your feedback so we can continue improving it.

What we stand for

Four things we never compromise on.

Safety first, always

Every feature — from school email verification to background checks to post-match-only messaging — exists because safety is not a checkbox. It's the whole point.

Community before scale

Assistly is for Indian Hill. Not eventually Indian Hill — now. We are not trying to be in every city. We are trying to be indispensable to one community first.

Students deserve real work

Part-time work builds character, financial literacy, and confidence. We take it seriously that students are trusting this platform to connect them with real, fair opportunities.

Families deserve peace of mind

When a family hires through Assistly, they should feel certain they've done everything right. That certainty is what we are building toward with every safeguard we add.
