About Assistly

Safe. Local. Built for Indian Hill.

Assistly is a private job-matching platform connecting Indian Hill families with school-verified students. Every user is a member of your community.

Indian Hill Exempted Village School District

Our mission

Build trust between neighbors before a dollar is exchanged.

Most platforms designed for odd jobs are built for strangers. Assistly is built on the opposite premise: that the most reliable person to help your family is someone who already lives in your neighborhood, goes to school nearby, and has their reputation on the line within that same community.

Every safeguard — school email verification, admin approval, background checks — exists to make that trust explicit, so families can hire with confidence and students can work with dignity.

Our story

How Assistly came to be.

From a simple idea to a purpose-built platform for the Indian Hill community.

The problem

Finding trusted local help is harder than it should be.

Families in close-knit communities want to hire local students they can trust — but there was no easy, safe way to make that connection. Word of mouth only goes so far.

The insight

Trust already exists — it just needed a platform.

Indian Hill is a tight community. The trust is already there. Assistly just makes it accessible — giving families and students a structured, safe way to act on it.

The build

Purpose-built for one district, not scaled down from everywhere.

Assistly was designed from the ground up for the Indian Hill School District — not retrofitted from a national platform. Every feature reflects the specific needs of this community.

Today

A live platform, exclusive to Indian Hill.

Assistly is now open to Indian Hill families and students. Sign-ups are open, every account is reviewed, and the platform is actively moderated to stay safe and community-first.

What we stand for

Four things we never compromise on.

01

Safety first, always

Every feature — from school email verification to background checks to post-match-only messaging — exists because safety is not a checkbox. It's the whole point.

02

Community before scale

Assistly is for Indian Hill. Not eventually Indian Hill — now. We are not trying to be in every city. We are trying to be indispensable to one community first.

03

Students deserve real work

Part-time work builds character, financial literacy, and confidence. We take it seriously that students are trusting this platform to connect them with real, fair opportunities.

04

Families deserve peace of mind

When a family hires through Assistly, they should feel certain they've done everything right. That certainty is what we are building toward with every safeguard we add.
