Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Assistly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially minors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using Assistly, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Category Examples How collected
Account information Name, email address, phone number, password (hashed) You provide this at signup
Profile data Age, bio, skills, profile photo, zip code You provide this optionally
Job and activity data Job listings created, applications submitted, jobs completed, reviews given and received Generated through platform use
Messages In-app messages between students and families Sent through the platform
Location data General location (zip code) used for job distance calculations. Precise GPS is optional. You provide or grant permission
Device data Push notification token, device OS version Collected automatically when you register for notifications
Usage data App interactions, error logs Collected automatically

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Assistly platform
  • Verify that students hold valid @ihsd.us school email addresses
  • Match students with family job postings by location and category
  • Deliver push notifications about job activity, messages, and platform updates
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, application updates)
  • Send occasional founder-voice emails for users who have not yet completed a job (opt-out available by replying to any email)
  • Maintain safety, detect fraud, and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Provide parental monitoring features to linked family accounts

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Assistly is available to users as young as 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

For users between 13 and 17 years old:

  • We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the platform.
  • We do not sell or share personal information of minors with third parties for advertising purposes.
  • Parents or guardians may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's information by emailing support@assistlyconnect.org.
  • The optional parental monitoring feature allows a linked family account to view a student's message history. Students must confirm the link before monitoring begins.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With other users: Your public profile (name, photo, rating, skills, bio) is visible to other approved users on the platform. Your address and contact details are never shared publicly.
  • Service providers: We use third-party providers to operate the platform, including Supabase (database and authentication), Render (hosting), Resend (transactional email), and Apple Push Notification Service / Firebase (push notifications). These providers process data only on our behalf.
  • Safety and legal compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, to protect the safety of a user or the public, or to respond to a valid legal request.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred. You will be notified before any such transfer occurs.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it longer.

6. Push Notifications

If you grant permission, Assistly sends push notifications about job activity, new messages, and application updates. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device's system settings or within the Assistly app.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information through the app's Edit Profile feature
  • Delete your account and associated data at any time through the app (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Opt out of non-transactional emails by replying to any email with "unsubscribe"
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing support@assistlyconnect.org

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Contact us to exercise those rights.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Assistly website uses minimal cookies necessary to operate the site. We do not use third-party advertising trackers, behavioral profiling, or cross-site tracking technologies.

9. Third-Party Links

The platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third parties, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email, and update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of Assistly after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
