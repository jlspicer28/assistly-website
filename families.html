Getting started

Four steps to your first hire.

01

Create an account

Sign up with any email. Families may be asked to complete a background check.

02

Post a task

Add the date, time, and pay for babysitting, yard work, errands, or tutoring.

03

Review applicants

See profiles from school-verified students who applied, and pick the best fit.

04

Connect and pay

Message safely in-app, then arrange payment directly once the job is done.
