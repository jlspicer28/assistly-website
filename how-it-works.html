How it works

Up and running in four steps.

Designed to be safe at every stage — not just at signup. Here's exactly what happens from account creation to a completed job.

01

Create your account

Students sign up with their @ihsd.us email. Families use any email. Every account is reviewed by an admin before access is granted.

02

Families post tasks

Post babysitting, yard work, errands, or tutoring with the date, time, and pay. Background-checked families only.

03

Students browse and apply

Approved students browse open tasks and apply for the ones that match their schedule and skills.

04

Get matched, connect safely

When a family selects a student, in-app messaging opens. Payment is arranged directly between them.

Safety

Every safeguard, explained.

Safety isn't a feature — it's the foundation Assistly is built on.

Admin-approved accounts

Every student and family is manually reviewed before they can access the platform. No anonymous users, ever.

School email verification

Students must sign up with their @ihsd.us email, confirming they're a current member of the district.

Background-checked families

Families complete a third-party background check before they're allowed to post tasks or contact students.

Post-match messaging only

Students and families can't contact each other until a match is confirmed — all messages stay on-platform.

Report and flag system

Any user, post, or message can be flagged. Admins review every report and can remove content immediately.

Parent consent for minors

Students who need parental consent must have signed forms on file before their account is activated.
